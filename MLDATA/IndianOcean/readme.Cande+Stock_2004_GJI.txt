Magnetic anomaly identifications from: Cande, S. C., and J. M. Stock (2004), Pacific-Antarctic-Australia motion and the formation of the Macquarie Plate, Geophys. J. Int., 157, 399-414. 

This 'Indian' set includes magnetic anomaly identifications west of the George V fracture zone.
More magnetic anomaly identifications from Cande and Stock (2004) can be found in the 'Pacific Ocean Reconstruction Data' compilation.
