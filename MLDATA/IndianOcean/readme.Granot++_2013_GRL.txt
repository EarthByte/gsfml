Magnetic anomaly identifications from: Granot, R., Cande, S. C., Stock, J. M., and Damaske, D., 2013, Revised Eocene-Oligocene kinematics for the West Antarctic rift system, Geophys. Res. Lett., 40, 279-284. 

This 'Indian' set includes magnetic anomaly identifications west of the George V fracture zone.
More magnetic anomaly identifications from Granot et al. (2013) can be found in the 'Pacific Ocean Reconstruction Data' compilation.
